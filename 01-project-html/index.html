Scientific Name:

[ WARAWARA ]

Discovered by / at:

Mahito, yesterday

Family:

weird little guys

Habitat:

Sea World- alternate plane of existence in Hayao Miyazaki's The Boy and the Heron.

Appearance:

Cute, white, and bloblike spirits who are usually smiling. Usually move fluidly in masses. Can walk, run, jump, tumble, and float.

Key Characteristics:

  • Cute and bloblike
  • Eats fish guts to mature.
  • Will fly up to try to be born.
  • Predators: pelicans

What do we know so far about the creature?

These cute WARAWARA, what is their story? The warawara are the unborn human souls of the Sea World in The Boy and the Heron. They desire to fly up and enter Mahito's world: to be born as humans. On the way, they are sometimes eaten by Pelicans.

How do we interact with this creature?

Feed FISH GUTS to mature. When they mature, they will fly up. Eventually, shoot fireworks to ward of the Pelicans and help the warawara complete their journey.
